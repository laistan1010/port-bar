DOCKED

TERRACE

PORT is a New York–style resto-bar built inside a shipping container aesthetic — a tribute to Hong Kong's identity as one of the world's busiest ports. Steel, timber, Edison bulbs. The kind of bar that doesn't try hard, because it doesn't have to.
